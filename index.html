Encore
Luxury Venue
Bathroom Pods

The porta-potty is one of the most degrading experiences in modern life. A hot plastic box, a line of 40 people, a horrible smell, and you miss half the show getting there and back. It doesn't have to be this way.

Encore is a luxury bathroom pod system designed for concerts, festivals, and major events. We bring in a trailer with a suite of private, self-contained, hotel-grade bathroom pods. But the magic isn't just the hardware ‚Äî it's the system.

Open the app. Join the virtual queue. Go back and enjoy the show. When your pod is ready, you get a push notification and a one-time unlock code sent directly to your phone. Walk up, unlock, and you have five uninterrupted minutes of a genuinely beautiful, fully private bathroom experience.

Because users are on accounts with verified identities and a reputation rating, we can guarantee cleanliness and appropriate use ‚Äî making it possible to offer a premium experience that stays premium.

GottaGo
City Bathroom
Network

Finding a clean, accessible bathroom in a city is a surprisingly hard problem. Public restrooms are scarce, often unsafe, and frequently locked. Coffee shops require a purchase. It's a daily friction that affects everyone ‚Äî especially people who are traveling, elderly, or managing health conditions.

GottaGo is a subscription network that turns existing business bathrooms into a city-wide clean bathroom system. For $10/month, members get access to a vetted network of partnered businesses ‚Äî coffee shops, bookstores, retailers ‚Äî that have agreed to let GottaGo members use their facilities, no purchase required.

Businesses win too. Every bathroom visit is a foot traffic opportunity. A GottaGo member walking into Powell's to use the bathroom might walk out with a book. And because every member is verified and rated, business owners aren't taking a risk ‚Äî our reputation system means only good actors get access.
